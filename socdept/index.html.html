About Department

The Department of Sociology was established to promote academic excellence in the study of social institutions and relationships. Our vision is to foster critical thinking, research, and social responsibility among students.

Vision

  • Promote social awareness and responsibility
  • Encourage research and innovation

Mission

  • Provide quality teaching in sociology
  • Engage students in community-oriented research
  • Uphold ethical academic practices

Courses Offered

  • Undergraduate Program (BA in Sociology) - 3 years
  • Postgraduate Program (MA in Sociology) - 2 years
  • PhD Program in Sociology - Duration as per university norms
